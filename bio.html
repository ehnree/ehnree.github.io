BIOGRAPHY
My name is Henry Zhou. I was born in YiFeng, China. Growing up, my family moved around a lot.
Some of the places I have lived in include China, Canada, and Japan. I moved to the United
States when I was 4 years old, and I have resided in Massachusetts ever since. I attended
Thompson Elementary School in Arlington, went to RJ Grey Jr High School in Acton, and
I attended Acton Boxborough Regional High School.

Currently, I am a sophomore at Tufts University where I am pursuing a degree
in computer science. When I am not coding, I spend my time hurdling for
the Tufts Track and Field team. I also enjoy listening to music, cooking, and
playing video games.
